Goals

The web site should produce an increase of customers of at least 50%. We get players of all ages, but mostly they are 35 – 50. We want the site to appeal to a younger audience, getting kids playing while they’re in high school (15 – 30). The success of the web site will be measured by the number of customers that sign up on the web page.

Target Audience

Younger audience between the ages 15 to 30. Preferrably starting in high school.
